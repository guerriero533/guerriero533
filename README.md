# Hi, I'm Francesco 👋🔭
Graduate MSc Astronomy student at Leiden University  

**Research interests:**
- _Stellar Evolution Theory_: Evolution of massive binary stars, focusing on life cycles, stellar interactions, tidal
disruption events, nuclear processes in interiors, instability mechanisms (e.g., photodisintegration), culminating
in core-collapse and the formation of their remnants. Fascinated by the connection between observational
signatures and theoretical constraints and by integrated approach combining observations with numerical
simulations to advance the understanding of binary evolution.
- _Gravitational-Wave Physics_: Formation and evolution of gravitational waves and their compact progenitors,
including the role of relativistic effects.
- _Applied General Relativity_: Metric perturbations, compact-object formation and evolution, inflation theory, and
simulations.
